BRAC Local Government Records - Offline Ordering

These records can be ordered in person through BRAC.  Email us to order records at brac@ncgrl.vic.gov.au.  To discuss and order in person you can speak to one of our helpful staff on Wednesdays or Thursdays by calling (03) 5449 2700.

NOTE: A small number of public records at BRAC have not yet been listed on the Public Record Office Victoria (PROV) online catalogue, and have not been allocated a Victorian Public Record Series Number (VPRS).  These records can't be ordered online and must be ordered directly.

VPRS = Victorian Public Record Series Number.  All records on the PROV Catalogue have a VPRS Number.
