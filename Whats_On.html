What's On

W.C. Vahland Exhibit

We are curating a special exhibit highlighting William Charles Vahland’s extensive career.  Our displays explore his correspondence to the former Sandhurst/Bendigo Council, related Bendigo Regional Archives Centre records as well as artefacts on loan from the Bendigo Art Gallery and Bendigo Library. 

The Exhibit will be showing from May-July 2016, 1st floor Bendigo Library.
