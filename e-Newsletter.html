Subscribe for quarterly e-Newsletters

Contact BRAC brac@ncgrl.vic.gov.au with a request to subscribe to the new Bendigo Regional Archives Centre (BRAC) quarterly e-newsletter called Bric-a-BRAC.  The first issue was published in May 2014. 

Subscribers will receive each issue of Bric-a-BRAC via email as well as notices about upcoming events, new collections or records becoming available.

Bric-a-BRAC offers an overview of what's happening in the world of BRAC and highlights details of one or two records or collections of particular interest so that over time you can discover our historical gems.  We believe our collection is unique and this is a way for you to discover information you cannot find anywhere else.

To Unsubscribe

If you would like to cease receiving the e-newsletter simply email BRAC brac@ncgrl.vic.gov.au and ask to unsubscribe from the e-newsletter mailing list.
