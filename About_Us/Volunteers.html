Volunteers

If you are interested in volunteering for the Bendigo Regional Archives Centre please email BRAC brac@ncgrl.vic.gov.au

Currently there are a number of volunteers assisting with transcribing 19th Century Petitions and typing the petition content.

We welcome your interest in volunteering.  We will register your interest in volunteering with the Bendigo Regional Archives Centre and keep your contact details and take into account your area of interest. We will contact you should a volunteering opportunity become available.
