Visit Us

The Bendigo Regional Archives Centre (BRAC) Reading Room, on the first floor of the Bendigo Library, can be visited on Wednesdays and Thursdays from 10.00am to 4.30pm.


See BRAC Guide 1 for information about visiting and the service, or go to Visit the Archives for further information.
